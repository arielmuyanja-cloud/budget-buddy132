❓ Common questions

What makes the AI coach worth paying for?

Every plan gets unlimited AI coach access — it's not a chatbot bolted onto a tracker. From Plus upward, it starts reaching out to you proactively instead of just answering questions when asked.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime — changes apply from your next billing cycle.

Is my bank data safe?

Bank connections never store your bank login — that's handled entirely by a secure third-party provider. Budget Buddy only ever sees transaction data, never your credentials.

What happens if my team outgrows a plan?

You'll get a heads-up before hitting your seat or account limit, with a one-click upgrade to the next tier.
