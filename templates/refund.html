Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to be happy with Budget Buddy. This policy explains how refunds work for our subscription plans.

1. Monthly subscriptions

Budget Buddy plans are billed monthly with no long-term contract. You may cancel at any time, and you will retain access to your plan's features until the end of the current billing period. We do not provide prorated refunds for the unused portion of a billing period.

2. Requesting a refund

If you believe you were charged in error, or you're unsatisfied with the Service within the first 14 days of a new subscription, contact us at amuyanja1314@gmail.com with your account username and the reason for your request. We review refund requests on a case-by-case basis.

3. Payment processing

All payments are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Approved refunds are issued back to your original payment method through Paddle and may take several business days to appear, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. Exceptions

We reserve the right to decline refund requests that appear to be abusive of this policy, such as repeated subscribe-refund cycles.

5. Contact

For any billing questions, reach out to amuyanja1314@gmail.com.
