Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Budget Buddy ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information we collect

When you use Budget Buddy, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, username, and password (stored securely)
  • Financial data you enter: income, expenses, categories, and savings goals
  • Usage data: pages visited and features used, via Google Analytics
  • Payment information: handled entirely by our payment processor, Paddle.com — we never see or store your card details

2. How we use your information

We use your information to provide and improve the Service, including generating AI financial suggestions based on your entered income and spending, processing subscription payments, and communicating with you about your account.

3. AI features

Questions you ask the AI Coach, along with relevant financial summary data, are sent to a third-party AI provider to generate a response. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service providers necessary to run Budget Buddy, such as our payment processor (Paddle) and our hosting provider (Render), each of whom is bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

5. Data storage

Your data is stored on our hosting provider's servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can also delete individual expenses, income entries, and goals directly within the app.

7. Children's privacy

Budget Buddy is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
